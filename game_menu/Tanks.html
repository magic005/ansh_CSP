Instructions

This is a 2 player game, where each player controls one tank. Each tank can shoot a bullet with a cooldown, and the aim of the game is to kill the other tank before they kill yours.

Player 1

Press Q to Shoot.
Use WASD to move.

Player 2

Press / to Shoot.
Use Arrow Keys to move.
